Lecture 6 Bass Model and Forecasting

Sales forecasting is an interesting problem. When companies realized they have data that are of potential values to business, one of the most common first usage of such data potential is for forecasting.
In this course, we are going to discuss a very special type of forecasting model, the BASS model.
The Bass model is designed for only new products. It answers the forecasting question from a slighly different angle. Forecasting usually answers how many products will be sold in each time period. Bass model think it from the customer adoption perspective.
