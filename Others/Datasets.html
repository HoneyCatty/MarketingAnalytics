Datasets on Team Projects

For this class, you are required to work in a group to finish a marketing analytics project. Each team should identify a project, obtain access to the relevant data, define a research question, analyze the data, and present the project to the class.

Detailed instuction information is on:

Instructions on Team Projects

Current available Datasets are:

  • CDurableGoods
  • SCU_reunionData
  • SVB_data
  • TabletData

For TabletData, a large datafile arsedcontent.zip should be downloaded from https://github.com/HoneyCatty/MarketingAnalytics/releases.
