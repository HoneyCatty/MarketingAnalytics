  <h1>Welcome to Marketing Analytics Course</h1>

  <p>This course is designed in response to the need for analytic talent in the marketplace. The overarching purpose of this course is to convey the benefits of a systematic, analytical approach to marketing decision-making, and to build skills, knowledge and confidence in undertaking such analyses.  </p>

  <p>

This course is not a statistic or math course, focusing on pure technical details and equation derivations. It is a Marketing Analytics course, aiming to prepare future managers who<br> 

(1) appreciate the importance of competitive advantages leveraged by analytics; <br>

(2) understand the existence of the tools, the advantages and limitations of each tool; <br> 

(3) can apply these tools, interpret the input and communicate the output from these tools and models, and apply them to assist business decisions. 

 </p>
